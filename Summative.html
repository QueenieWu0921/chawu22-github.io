Stray Dogs
About This
Website
This website is about stray dogs for people above 10 years
old. The website will contain important information such as
where the stray dogs usually come from, consequences of
having too many stray dogs, and the solution to decrease
the amount of stray dogs. The purpose I’m making this
website is to hope people to be aware of the problem of
stray dogs and to tell them that dogs are not a toy for them,
they’re here to be taken care, not to be abandoned by
humans. Hope you enjoy reading my website! :)

Consequences
Some consequences if we don’t stop this problem are: dogs
get hit by cars easily while wandering on streets, and the dirty
living conditions can make them ill and really painful. Also, if
there are too many dogs, the government might start the
"Euthanasia Progam", which they'll inject medicinces into the
dogs' body. Then the dogs will slowly die without pain.
Solutions
To solve this problem, people need to have more thoughtful minds before they actually
buy a dog, they need to think all over if they have the ability to take care of them, and
whether they have the determined mind to take care them until they die. Also, people
should try not to buy the dogs in a dog store but adopt a stray dog so that the dog store
will stop trying to make more puppies on purpose to sell. Nowadays, the most popular
way the government decreases the amount of dog is to apply the CNR program to them
which includes killing stray dogs.
